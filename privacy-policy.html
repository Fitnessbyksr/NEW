Privacy Policy

How FitnessbyKSR collects, uses, and protects your personal data.

Last updated: April 2026

1. Who we are

FitnessbyKSR is operated by Kallum Rolfe, a personal trainer based at PT Factory, Denton, Manchester. We are the data controller for any personal data collected through this website.

Contact: ksrfitnessmanc@gmail.com

2. What data we collect

We collect personal data in the following ways:

  • Email sign-up (MailerLite): When you sign up for the free 5-day guide, we collect your first name and email address.
  • Contact form (Formspree): When you send a message via the contact page, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and message content.
  • Cookies: We use functional cookies to improve your experience on the site (see section 6 below).

3. How we use your data

  • To send you the free 5-day email guide you signed up for.
  • To send occasional follow-up emails related to fitness, our services, and useful content. You can unsubscribe at any time.
  • To respond to enquiries sent via the contact form.
  • We will never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Legal basis for processing

We process your personal data on the basis of consent (UK GDPR Article 6(1)(a)). By submitting the sign-up form or contact form, you give us permission to process your data for the purposes described above. You can withdraw consent at any time by unsubscribing from emails or contacting us directly.

5. Third-party services

We use the following trusted third-party services to process your data:

  • MailerLite - email marketing platform. Your email and name are stored on MailerLite's servers in accordance with their privacy policy.
  • Formspree - contact form processing. Messages submitted via our contact form are handled by Formspree.
  • Netlify / GitHub Pages - website hosting.

Each of these services has its own privacy policy and is compliant with applicable data protection law.

6. Cookies

This website uses the following cookies:

  • cookieConsent - stores your cookie preference (Accept or Decline). This is a functional cookie and does not track you.

We do not currently use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies. If this changes, we will update this policy and ask for your consent again.

7. How long we keep your data

We keep your email address and name for as long as you remain subscribed to our emails. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link at the bottom of any email. Contact form enquiries are kept for up to 12 months.

8. Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access - you can ask us what personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification - you can ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure - you can ask us to delete your data at any time.
  • Right to object - you can object to us processing your data.
  • Right to data portability - you can ask for a copy of your data in a portable format.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at ksrfitnessmanc@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Complaints

If you have concerns about how we handle your data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK's data protection authority, the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date at the top.

Questions about this policy? Email us at ksrfitnessmanc@gmail.com and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.
