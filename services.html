Who I work best with

I'm not for everyone — and that's fine. But if any of this sounds like you, we'll probably get on well.

😬

The nervous first-timer

You've never been to a gym, or haven't been in years. The idea feels overwhelming. You need somewhere that doesn't feel like everyone else knows the rules but you.

🔄

The returner

You've tried before — maybe several times. Different gyms, different programmes, different apps. Nothing's stuck. You're not lazy; you just haven't had the right support yet.

🧡

The older adult

You're in your 40s, 50s or 60s and want to move better, feel stronger, and actually enjoy exercise. No boot camps. No being made to feel like you're "too late."

You know exercise helps your mental health, you just can't seem to make it stick. You need someone who understands both sides — the physical and the emotional.

The weight loss goal

You want to lose weight and keep it off — but without crash diets or punishing routines. Sustainable progress, built around your actual life.

🏃

The busy person

You've got a job, a family, a life. You need training that fits around your schedule — not the other way around. Short, effective sessions that actually work.
