If that's not you — and honestly, it's not most people — you've probably tried and quit more times than you want to admit. And each time, a little voice says: *maybe I'm just not a gym person.*

That voice is lying to you.

You're not lazy. You don't lack willpower. You haven't failed.

You just haven't had the right person in your corner yet.
