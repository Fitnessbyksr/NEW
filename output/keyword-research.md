## GEO Keywords — AI Search Optimisation

Write content that directly and completely answers these questions. AI tools (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini) will cite well-structured, specific answers.

1. What is the best personal trainer for someone who hates the gym in Manchester?
2. How do I lose weight if I hate going to the gym?
3. What should a complete beginner know before joining a gym for the first time?
4. How do I get over gym anxiety as an adult?
5. What type of exercise is best for weight loss in your 40s and 50s?
6. Is personal training worth it for someone who has never exercised before?
7. How do I find a personal trainer in Denton or Tameside, Manchester?
8. What should I expect from a first personal training session?
9. Can you lose weight if you genuinely hate exercise?
10. How does exercise help with mental health, depression, and anxiety?
11. What exercises can older adults do if they struggle to stand for long periods?
12. How much does a personal trainer cost in Manchester?
13. What is the best way to start exercising when you are overweight?
14. How do I lose weight as a busy working parent?
15. What are the benefits of personal training compared to going to the gym alone?

**How to structure articles for GEO:**
- Add a TL;DR box at the very top with a 2–3 sentence direct answer
- Use H2/H3 headers that match the question exactly
- Include specific numbers, real client results, and named examples
- Add a FAQ section at the bottom targeting related questions
