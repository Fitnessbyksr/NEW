Sound familiar?

If you're new to this, returning after years away, or just not sure where to start — you're in exactly the right place. Most people feel that way before they come in. That's kind of the point.

😬

You've joined a gym and barely gone, because it felt overwhelming

🚗

You've driven to the gym and turned back without going in

😕

You feel like everyone else got a manual you never received

💭

You've tried to "get fit first" before getting help. It never works.

That's not a character flaw. It's a design problem.

I was a PCSO for two years, then a police officer for three years. When I left, my mental health was in a bad place and I'd stopped looking after myself completely. I joined a big commercial gym and quit after three weeks. Going home just felt easier than going in.

That experience changed everything. It's why I became a PT, and exactly who I work with.
