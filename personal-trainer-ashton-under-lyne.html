A private gym, not a public one

The PT Factory isn't a commercial gym. You're not walking in alongside strangers doing their own workouts. It's a private training space — and for anyone who's ever felt self-conscious in a gym, that distinction is everything.

Actually built for beginners

Most gyms put personal trainers in the corner of a building that was designed for people who already know what they're doing. The PT Factory is different. Every session starts where you actually are — not where a generic programme assumes you should be. The drive from Ashton to Denton is straightforward — mostly across Tameside with free parking when you arrive. No city centre congestion, no multi-storey.
